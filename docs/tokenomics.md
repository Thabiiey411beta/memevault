# MemeVault Tokenomics

## $MEPL Token
- **Total Supply**: 2,000,000,000 $MEPL
- **Distribution**:
  - 40% (800M): Liquidity incentives and airdrops (circulating)
  - 30% (600M): Treasury (50% or 300M circulating initially)
  - 20% (400M): Team and advisors (vested, non-circulating)
  - 10% (200M): Community missions and governance (circulating)
- **Initial Circulating Supply**: 1,000,000,000 $MEPL (800M + 200M)
- **Burn Mechanism**:
  - **Phase 1**: 12% monthly burn until circulating supply reaches 500,000,000 $MEPL (~6 months).
  - **Phase 2**: 3% monthly burn until circulating supply reaches 100,000,000 $MEPL (~51 months, total ~4.75 years).
  - **Final Circulating Supply**: 100,000,000 $MEPL
  - **Final Total Supply**: 800,000,000 $MEPL (100M circulating + 700M non-circulating)
- **Purpose**: Governance, staking rewards, mission incentives
- **Impact**: Deflationary model increases scarcity, potentially boosting value 2-3x (e.g., $0.1 to $0.3)

## Revenue Streams
- **Trading Fees**: 0.1-1% on pool swaps ($3,000/day on $1M TVL)
- **Network Fees**: 10-20% of Supra fees ($1,000/day)
- **Cross-Chain Yields**: 10-15% APY ($7,500/year on $50,000)
- **Treasury**: Funds development, marketing, and IL protection ($109,500/year)
